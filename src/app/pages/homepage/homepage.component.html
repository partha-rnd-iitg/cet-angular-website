Center for Educational Technology

Introduction

CET at IIT Guwahati, established in 2002, leads national and regional education projects like NPTEL and TEQIP, collaborating with IITs and state departments. It offers advanced infrastructure for multidisciplinary mission projects using educational technologies.

Vision

The vision and mission of CET are to achieve the highest synergy between Education, Human Development and Technology through Collective Research, Design & Development of educational/pedagogical content, Educational technology and Collaborative Interdisciplinary team work.
